Who We Are

Born from the impact of violence in South Central Los Angeles, L.A.U.R.A. (Life After Uncivil Ruthless Acts) was created to transform loss into action. Grounded in lived experience and compassion, we walk alongside families through grief, empower youth toward safer futures, and bring vital resources directly to the community.

Our Impact

  • Supporting families through crisis and grief since 2007
  • Engaging youth in prevention and empowerment programs
  • Connecting thousands of residents to vital resources each year
  • Mobilizing a community committed to peace, healing, and hope
Our Programs

LAURA's Victim
Support Program

The Victims Support Program aims to create strength out of tragedy, and unity out of pain. We act as a bridge between families who have suffered devastating loss and existing resources. We take a holistic approach — offering support to the family of the victim and the family of the perpetrator, connecting suffering families to concrete help and creating stronger, healthier communities.

Guidance

We offer guidance in navigating the system to get families the resources that are available.

Emotional Support

We offer a space where victims' family members can share and grieve without judgment.

Financial Support

We mobilize help from family, community, and our network of contacts.

Crisis Responders

We show up at the scene offering essential needs — safe space, water, clothing, meds — and serve as liaison with law enforcement.

Monthly Support Groups

A safe space for loved ones to talk, celebrate, mourn, and heal alongside others who understand the same pain.

Youth Program

We help youth in affected families process pain and lay down the burden of having a loved one murdered or incarcerated.

Youth Program

L.A.U.R.A Youth Program

A positive organization that rose out of the streets of South Central L.A. — bringing youth together through workshops, trips, and civic engagement every Thursday at 5 PM.
