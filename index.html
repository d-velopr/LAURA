Rooted in healing, prevention, and collective action.

L.A.U.R.A. is a South Central Los Angeles-based nonprofit dedicated to supporting families impacted by gang and gun violence, empowering youth, and mobilizing the community to break cycles of harm through healing, prevention, and collective action.

Our Impact

Our work centers on three connected branches that help families heal, youth grow, and communities organize for lasting safety.

Crisis Response & Family Healing

When violence takes a life, L.A.U.R.A. responds with compassion and care.

  • On-site crisis response and family support when requested
  • Fundraising assistance for funeral and memorial expenses
  • Connections to grief counseling and mental health resources
  • Ongoing guidance for victim-survivors and loved ones

Youth Empowerment

Empowerment begins with opportunity, connection, and belief in our youth.

  • Youth clubs and safe spaces
  • Paid internships
  • Educational workshops and life-skills programming
  • Mentorship and gang-prevention/intervention initiatives
  • Adventures and activities that expand horizons
  • Mental health services

Civic Mobilization

L.A.U.R.A. brings the community together to heal, organize, and take action.

  • Civic engagement and leadership development
  • Community collaboration and partnerships
  • Trust-building between residents and local institutions
  • Seasonal events and our signature annual resource fair

Annual Event

Annual Peace Walk & Community Resource Fair

Each March, L.A.U.R.A. hosts the Annual Peace Walk & Community Resource Fair, a free, family-centered event that draws over 2,000 children, youth, and adults to Fred Roberts Park in South Central Los Angeles.

The Peace Walk commemorates the lives of murdered victims to violence while affirming our collective commitment to healing and change. Following the walk, the park becomes a hub of connection and celebration, featuring:

  • Community and health resources
  • Local organizations and vendors
  • Youth activities and family programming
  • Live entertainment, special guests, food, and raffles

All net proceeds from the event directly support L.A.U.R.A.’s year-round programs and victim-survivor support.
