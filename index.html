L.A.U.R.A 501(3)(c)

L.A.U.R.A. is a non-profit organization dedicated to enhancing the quality of life of the residents of South Central Los Angeles. We accomplish this by empowering youth, victim-survivors, and their families through civic awareness; facilitating community collaboration; and bringing a wide range of resources to the community.

Our Impact

1. We bring resources to families in need.

Every month we host bi-weekly civic engagement and youth meetings. Annually, we host job fairs, workshops on resume building, and collaborate with organizations that provide free medical exams, immigration assistance, and more!

2. We Educate The Youth!

We educate youth on multiple subjects that will allow them to thrive. From mental and emotional well-being to eating healthy and understanding finances. We also teach them on what healthy relationships look like. Our youth also participate in summer camping trips and community gardening opportunities. We believe the youth are the future and your donations will allow us to maintain our current activities while allowing us to bring in new workshops, speakers and addiitonal opportunities for growth.
