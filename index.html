Join The Movement

We bring resources to families in need

Every month we host bi-weekly civic engagement and youth meetings. Annually, we host job fairs, workshops on resume building, and collaborate with organizations that provide free medical exams, immigration assistance, and more!

Contact Us

We Educate The Youth!

We educate youth on multiple subjects that will allow them to thrive. From mental and emotional well-being to eating healthy and understanding finances. We also teach them on what healthy relationships look like. Our youth also participate in summer camping trips and community gardening opportunities. We believe the youth are the future and your donations will allow us to maintain our current activities while allowing us to bring in new workshops, speakers and additional opportunities for growth.

We Work Together

We Listen, We Bring Resources, We Support

Since 2003, L.A.U.R.A. has helped over 50+ families who were victims of homicide with emotional support, connecting them to city resources and healing. Our L.A.U.R.A. Victim Support Program and L.A.U.R.A. Youth Program have contributed resources to support families after traumatic events.
