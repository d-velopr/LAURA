We Support

L.A.U.R.A (Los Angeles Reentry Health Advisory Collaborative) is a California non-profit organization that is dedicated to supporting individuals transitioning from incarceration back into society. Our mission is to provide comprehensive health services, resources, and advocacy to ensure successful reentry and reduce recidivism rates.

At L.A.U.R.A, we believe that everyone deserves a second chance. We work closely with local communities, healthcare providers, and government agencies to create a supportive network that addresses the unique challenges faced by formerly incarcerated individuals.

Our programs include mental health counseling, substance abuse treatment, job training, and housing assistance. We also offer educational workshops and support groups to empower our clients and help them build a brighter future.

Join us in our mission to transform lives and create a more just and equitable society. Together, we can make a difference.

Our Programs

L.A.U.R.A offers a range of programs designed to support individuals during their reentry journey. Our services include:

  • Mental Health Counseling
  • Substance Abuse Treatment
  • Job Training and Employment Assistance
  • Housing Support and Resources
  • Educational Workshops and Support Groups

Each program is tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients, ensuring they receive the support necessary for a successful transition back into society.
