Our Policy Impact

Through our advocacy and participation in key county initiatives, L.A.U.R.A has helped shape policies that prioritize community wellness, racial equity, and alternatives to incarceration.

Advocacy Focus Areas

  • Alternatives to incarceration
  • Racial equity in justice systems
  • Community-based reentry programs
  • Victim support services
  • Youth empowerment initiatives

Policy Outcomes

  • Increased funding for community programs
  • Enhanced reentry support services
  • Greater community representation in policy decisions
  • Improved access to resources for affected families
  • Stronger collaborations with county agencies
